# ‚öîÔ∏è QUESTLOG: THE GILDED CHRONICLE

**QuestLog** is a premium, AI-driven RPG application designed to transform the mundane struggle of real-life productivity into an epic legendary saga. It gamifies your existence, turning every habit, chore, and project into a heroic quest that grants you actual Experience (XP), Gold (G), and permanent entries in your heroic legacy.

---

## üåå Core Vision
The world is full of distractions that feel like "side quests." QuestLog flips the narrative‚Äîmaking your real-life goals the **Main Quest**. Level up your character by leveling up your life.

---

## üî• Key Features

### üé≠ 1. Legendary Character Creation (Chronicler)
- **Class System**: Choose from five unique classes‚ÄîWarrior, Mage, Rogue, Scholar, or Creator. Each has its own primary attributes, lore, and visual aesthetic.
- **AI Bio Transformation**: The **Chronicler AI** takes your simple background story and rewrites it into a class-specific, cinematic origin story.
- **Premium Portrait Gallery**: Choose from a grid of high-definition, class-appropriate character portraits crafted for a professional RPG look.

### üìú 2. The Quest Board (Oracle AI)
- **Bounty System**: Post a simple, real-world task (e.g., "Clean the kitchen").
- **AI Transformation**: The **Oracle AI** (powered by Groq llama-3.3) transforms your "Bounty" into a thematic "Quest" with narrative steps, difficulty ratings, and calculated rewards.
- **Rewards**: Earn Gold and XP upon completion. High-difficulty "Legendary" quests grant massive wealth and fame.

### üï∞Ô∏è 3. Communion with the Future (Temporal Link)
- **AI Future Self**: Chat with a version of yourself from the year 2036. They have perfect memory of your deeds and provide "Temporal Briefings" on your dashboard.
- **Personality Customization**: Tune your Future Self to be a "Wise Mentor," a "Tough Love Coach," or a "Mystical Oracle."

### üìì 4. Record of Deeds (The Journal)
- **Permanent History**: Every completed quest is recorded as a **Deed**.
- **Life Analytics**: The AI looks at your "Chronicle Entries" to provide insights into your productivity patterns (e.g., "Persistence is at 92%").
- **Heatmap**: Track your daily consistency with a visual contribution map.
